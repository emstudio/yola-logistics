YOLA

Automated Logistics Finance & Reconciliation Platform

YOLA is a technology platform designed to automate financial reconciliation, transaction tracking, and trip-level profitability for logistics businesses. We integrate with banking systems and regulated financial infrastructure to provide secure, consent-based financial data access.

Core Capabilities:
  • Automated bank transaction reconciliation
  • Trip-level payment matching
  • Cashflow monitoring for logistics operations
  • Expense classification and analytics
  • Financial visibility for transporters and operators

Use of Financial Data

YOLA may access financial account information strictly through regulated and consent-based frameworks such as RBI-approved Account Aggregator systems or direct banking APIs.

Financial data is used solely for:

  • Transaction reconciliation
  • Financial analytics
  • Risk assessment (if applicable)
  • Operational automation

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security controls including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access control, and secure infrastructure practices. Data is processed only for the intended business purpose and never sold to third parties.

Privacy Policy

We collect only necessary operational and financial information required to provide our services. Users maintain control over their financial data and may revoke consent at any time where applicable under regulatory frameworks.
